A Tribute to [Konidela Pawan Kalyan]

Admirable Qualities

Kalyan founded a political party named Jana Sena Party on 14 March 2014

"Pawan Kalyan's talk touched my heart. My soul said that if there are youth like Pawan Kalyan, the Telugu spirit can never die. Telangana and Seemandhra can both prosper under someone like him."

Inspiration and Influence

Kalyan was invited to Harvard University to deliver a speech in 14th edition of India Conference 2017 along with R. Madhavan

The Jana Sena Party contested 21 constituencies in the 2024 Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly elections. Kalyan had contested in Pithapuram constituency. He won by a huge margin of more than 70,000 votes. The Jana Sena Party won in all 21 constituency and 2 loksabha seats where it had fielded it's candidates with 100% strike rate .[90]
